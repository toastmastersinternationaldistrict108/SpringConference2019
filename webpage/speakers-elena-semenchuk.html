Speaker Details

Elena Semenchuk

Elena Semenchuk is a Professional Coach, Trainer and Artist. In Toastmasters since 2009, ACG, ALB She organizes transformational trainings for women as well as trainings for creativity, effective feedback etc. One of her main beliefs is that we all are creative and talented and only need some effort, focus and attention to make our talents shine.

Effective Evaluation

Workshop language: English

Effective evaluation is a key to growth in public speaking. It can encourage, inspire, and motivate the speaker to improve, learn, be more creative and try new techniques. OR not. During this workshop we will practice evaluations and give a lot of feedback to each other. We will see what the key to effective evaluation is. We will share experience and have fun.
