Speaker Details

Lerio Cunha

Lerio was born and raised in Mozambique. He had lived 1 year in Berlin and now lives in Lisbon for the last 8 years. He is a Public Speaking Coach, Trainer and Keynote Speaker. He has the privilege to travel the world delivering training and speeches. Last year he had two major achievements: he did a Tour in the US and Canada (Chicago, New Orleans, DC, Buffalo, Ontario and New York) and won the 3rd place in the Western European Contest of Public Speaking in Athens. He dreams to be the Toastmasters World Champion of Public Speaking one day.

Conflict Resolution

Approach your conflict with the Nonviolent Communication tool.

As Toastmasters we know that the words we use and how we use them matter. And sometimes the way we communicate may cause certain conflicts. Conflict is present in our lives and there are two ways of seeing it: as a negative aspect or a positive one that will foster new opportunities. During this workshop we will see how to resolve using the Nonviolent Communication (NVC) tool. The NVC was developed by Marshal Rosenberg (1992).
