5 Feedback Models

Feedback is one of the basic factors building an agreement between people. As long as it’s adjusted to the level of acceptance and needs of feedback’s receiver. I’m inviting you to find 5 Feedback Models. Having that we will be able to choose the most adequate technique for particular situation. During the workshop every participant will have an opportunity to test different Feedback Models and learn their application options. Participants will get to know feedback techniques applicable in personal relationships, Toastmasters evaluations, in mentor-mentee relationship, in superordinate – subordinate business relationship and in trainer work. In order to build an agreement with another person consciously and effectively it’s good to know when and how should we provide feedback. I warmly invite you. Magdalena Kędzia.
