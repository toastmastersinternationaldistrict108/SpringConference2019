Speaker Details

Jerzy Zientkowski

Inspirational speaker, author, mentor, coach. Member of Professional Speakers Association in Poland, Vice-President 2011-2016.Member of Brian Tracy International Speakers Club. Talks to nearly 40 000 people yearly from conference stages, during workshops and while mentoring some of the biggest companies in Poland.

Toastmasters member, ACG, ALS, founder and mentor of five clubs. Five Times Polish champion of public speaking, in 2012 reached finals of European championship in Bonn.

He’s being regularly invited to biggest stages in Poland: TEDx, Spectacular Speaking, Kongres Business Intelligence, Diamenty Sprzedaży, Kongres Kadry and many more. Shares stage with some of the best polish and international speakers, such as Brian Tracy, Robert Cialdini, jacek Walkiewicz, prof. Jerzy Bralczyk, to name a few.

He spoke and mentored companies like Al Fianco Partners, Allegro, Allianz, Aviva, Aon Hewitt, BGK, Benefit, Bre Bank, Bricoman, BZ WBK…and that’s just the beginning of an alphabet.

Magnetic storytelling… straight from excel.

Workshop language: Polish

Magnetic storytelling… straight from excel" is a holistic approach to creation of presentations and speeches, using storytelling of course. You'll learn how to collect stories (and NOT invent them!), how to re-write them to make them better, when and how to use them. This will give you an on-stage advantage, eliminating stress and hesitation and leaving you with stories that work and charm your audience.
