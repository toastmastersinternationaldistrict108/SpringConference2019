Speaker Details

Kacper Biłozor

WHAT I DO?

I train middle and senior management, software developers, creative employees, and businessmen to speak in public, on small and large stages, both in Polish and in English. I conduct workshops and individual sessions, during which I take my clients from audience analysis and choosing the subject, through writing, selection of rhetorical devices, voice, body language, and stage movement preparation to the rehearsal on the day of the event.

MAJOR ACHIEVEMENTS:

• Two-time European Championships finalist in the humorous speeches competition.
• European Championships finalist in the inspirational speeches competition.
• European Championships semi-finalist in the impromptu speeches competition.
• European Championships semi-finalist in the speech evaluation competition.
• Poland's National champion in the impromptu speeches competition.
• Poland's National runner-up in the speech evaluation competition.
• I have brought four people six times to the podium of Poland's National Championships.
• This year my two mentees will compete in the finals of Poland's National Championships.
and in the finals of the Championships of Poland, Lithuania, Latvia, Estonia, and Finland.

EXPERIENCE:

• During the EUTop50 2017 I spoke in the European Parliament and prepared fifty start-up owners from all over Europe to pitch to three hundred investors and business angels.
• I prepared the CEO of a 3D printing start up to pitch at the New York Stock Exchange.
• I spoke at the Column Hall of Poland’s Parliament and co-managed communications during the last election campaign to the European Parliament.
